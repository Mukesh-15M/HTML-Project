Steps to register

  1. Find an Aadhaar Update Center
  2. Carry Necessary Documents
  3. Fill the Aadhaar Update/Correction Form
  4. Biometric Authentication
  5. Pay the Fee
  6. Receive Acknowledgment
  7. Wait for Update

Visit the official UIDAI website: https://uidai.gov.in

  • Go to "My Aadhaar" → Locate an Enrolment & Update Center" Find the nearest Aadhaar Seva Kendra or authorized center.
  • You only need your original Aadhaar card (or Aadhaar number). For mobile number update, usually no additional documents are required, but carry a valid Photo ID just in case.
  • At the center, request the Aadhaar Update/Correction Form. Fill in your Aadhaar number and the mobile number you wish to register/update.
  • Submit the form to the operator.
  • You will need to provide biometric verification (fingerprint or iris scan) to authenticate your identity. Pay the nominal fee (currently ₹50, but confirm at the center).
  • You will get an Update Request Number (URN) on an acknowledgment slip. Keep it safe to track your request.
  • The mobile number will usually be updated in 2–5 working days, but it may take up to 10 days in some cases. You’ll get an SMS confirmation on the registered mobile once the update is done. Go to UIDAI Update Status.Enter your Aadhaar number and URN to track the update.
